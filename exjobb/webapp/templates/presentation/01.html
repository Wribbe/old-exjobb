Diploma presentation pitch

Presenting the new and improved master diploma work.

Old pitch

I'll figure out the smallest possible design changes that will
enable the users of the Shotgun desktop-client to use it more efficiently.
Bright eyed and idealistic Stefan of 2018.

Problems with current angle

  • Little to no ways of customizing the Shotgun-web/-desktop client.
  • None of the current actual modifications are design related.
  • Boring questionnaires + poor answer rate ⟶ demotivating.
  • Excruciatingly long iteration loop.
  • Little to no real-world benefit or impact.
  • Resulting knowledge is specific and very niche.

New pitch

Our boss complains he has no easy way to see if any of his employees
assigned workload outstrips their scheduled work-capacity. This should be
possible to solve with a small- to medium-sized web-application.
Slightly jaded but hopefully more realistic Stefan of 2019.

Improvements with the new angle

  • Real-world benefits that impacts the whole team.
  • Single main stakeholder ⟶ tight iteration loop.
  • Questionnaires still possible, but no loger essential for advancing the project.
  • Major part of the work will be design related.
  • Large corpus of literature related to web-design best practices.
  • Resulting knowledge is in demand and is broad enough to be usable in a large swathe of disciplines.
