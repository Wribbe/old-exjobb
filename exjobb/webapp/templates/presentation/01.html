Avoiding stretching people too thin

Presenting a new pitch for my master diploma work

The problem

The Cinematic Producer wants a better way to see if any of his employees assigned workload is greater than their work-capacity.

Pitch to solve the problem

First, gather and parse data related to work-schedules and estimated project time from internal systems. Then, using well established web-technologies and design principles; build a small to medium sized web-application that display the data in such a way that it is easily to see if anyone is assigned work over their limit.

Why the change?

Problems with previous angle

  • Little to no way of customizing the Shotgun-web/-desktop client.
  • None of the modifications so far have been design related.
  • Very long iteration loop.
  • Little to no real-world benefit or impact.
  • Resulting knowledge is specific and very niche.

Improvements with the new angle

  • Real-world benefits that impacts the whole team.
  • Single main stakeholder ⟶ tight iteration loop.
  • Questionnaires no longer essential for advancing the project.
  • Major part of the work will be design related.
  • Large corpus of literature related to web-design best practices.
  • Resulting knowledge is in demand and broadly applicable.

What have I learnt?

Don't make it harder than it has to be

This new approach still uses the same core question: What design alterations have the largest impact?

But with the added benefits to the scientific evaluation:

  • Design is fully customizable ⟶ possible to implement and test any design approach.
  • Web-based technologies throughout ⟶ easier implementation and verification.
  • Web-hosted without need for Shotgun domain knowledge ⟶ large scale usability testing possible.

Make sure to be informed and keep on track

  • I did not know of the design limitations of Shotgun.
  • I did not fully define the projects scope and end-goal.
  • Too much time spent on things with no effect on final result; self-updating gantt-chart, etc.

Use all resources more efficiently

Time and focus

  • No more LATEX, final product web-based ⟶ presentation and article web-based.
  • Everything should tie back into the result ⟶ this presentation template used as basis for final presentation.

Communication

  • Host the current up-to-date version of presentation and article on the web and provide links, don't email or print pdfs.
  • Concisely define anything worthy of feedback in the presentation first. If it is approved, expand the concept in the article.
